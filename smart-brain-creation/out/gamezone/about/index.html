Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 30-08-2025



Introduction

Welcome to Hopscape! We respect your privacy and are committed to ensuring that your experience is safe and enjoyable. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you play the game.



Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data or ask for any permissions from players. Our game does not require you to create an account or provide any personal details.



Google Ads

Our game displays ads served by Google. These ads may collect non-personal information, such as device type and location, to deliver targeted advertising. Google’s use of cookies and other technologies allows it to serve ads based on your interaction with this game and other websites/apps.

For more information on how Google uses your information, you can review their Privacy Policy.



Data Usage

Since we do not collect any personal information, we do not use or share any of your data other than for ad-serving purposes as described above.



Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies for tracking purposes. However, Google Ads may use cookies to deliver more relevant ads based on user interaction.



Children’s Privacy

Our game is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 (or 16, depending on jurisdiction). If we discover that we have inadvertently collected personal data from a child, we will take immediate steps to delete it.



Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, feel free to contact us at:contact @smartbraincreations.com



Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here, and the date of the last revision will be updated.
